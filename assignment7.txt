What is the purpose of the doctype declaration?
-it tells the browser what version of html it should use to render the document.

What is the HTML element?
-it is the root element of the document every other element should be descendent of it.

What is the purpose of the head element?
-head element is where we put important meta-information about our webpages, and stuff required for our webpages to render correctly in the browser.

What is the purpose of the body element?
-this is whre all the content that will be displayed to users will go. the text,images,lists,links and so on.
